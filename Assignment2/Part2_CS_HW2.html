About CS Department

The Computer Science Department at Mason is the largest department in the College of Engineering and Computing, one of the largest departments on campus, and the largest, fastest growing, and best ranking Computer Science department in the Commonwealth of Virginia, with almost 80 full-time faculty members.

Undergraduate courses with the CS and SWE prefixes are available, as are graduate courses with the CS, INFS, ISA, and SWE prefixes.
The official descriptions for the CS department's courses may be found in the Mason catalogue.
